Welcome Address

I woke up one morning,tired of complaints about how things are not working and I asked my self, what can I do in my own little way to help my community?
I thought of creating a platform where people of like-minds regardless of religion, tribe or creed can come together in their own little ways to help their community through education, feeding, welfare etc for the less privileged. Thats how rethink the nation was born.
Feel free to post your suggestions on what you feel in your heart to achieve, let's build up a team and let's give hope to the needy.
Thank you.

Our Story

We are an organization set up to help the ,masses get educated as well as enhance the standard living. We seek to do this through donations and other pro bono services.

Our Mission

Welcome to Rethink, a platform that seeks to rethink and recreate ways to improve the lifestyle of people in the areas of education, business, and welfare. We are formulating in our own little way projects that seek to alleviate poverty and hopelessness caused by various reasons which is influencing the globe especially those in the underdeveloped world which includes Nigeria. Its also a platform to exchange ideas regardless of religion, tribe or creed that would help us to realize our potentials as a people.

Projects

All our project is centralized on the welfare of children, youths and businesses that would improve the standard of living for the masses.

Scholarship

We embark on scholarship and a meal a day for primary school students.

Skill acquisition

Creating a platform for skill acquisition for youths.

Partnership

Opening and partnering with other institutions involved in feeding the poor.
